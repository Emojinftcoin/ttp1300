3 Nights 4 Days Pattaya Getaway

Day 1: Arrival & Relaxation

Land in Bangkok and transfer to Pattaya. Check in to your hotel, relax at the beach, and enjoy an evening at the famous Walking Street.

Day 2: Island Adventure

Hop on a ferry to Koh Larn Island. Enjoy crystal-clear waters, optional water sports, and a serene beach vibe. Return to Pattaya by evening for the local night market buzz.

Day 3: Culture & Nightlife

Explore iconic landmarks like the Sanctuary of Truth or Mini Siam. Unwind with a traditional Thai massage and end your day with an optional cabaret show.

Day 4: Departure

Enjoy your morning coffee with a beach view before heading back to the airport with memories to last a lifetime.
